About Us

Diversified Spec Sales, Inc. is a manufacturer representative for commercial/industrial plumbing and washroom accessories. Our customers include end users, engineers, wholesalers and contractors.

With two warehouse office locations in Eastern and Western Michigan, Diversified Spec Sales, Inc. is able to respond to customer needs quickly and efficiently.

A customer-driven company, Diversified Spec Sales, Inc. has been in business since 1963, representing major lines since its inception.
